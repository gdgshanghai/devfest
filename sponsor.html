主办方

Shanghai GDG(originally GTUG) is the Google Developer Group in the largest city in China! It is the 3rd GDG established in Mainland China. It is a Chinese and English language based user group community for all developers, web masters, IT pros, students, and business people in Shanghai and the surrounding areas, to meet and share knowledge and experiences in using Google technologies and web development. The posting here is mostly in Chinese, but English postings are also welcome. In addition to this on-line community here, we will also organize community group meetings and events in Shanghai periodically, to discuss about Google technologies, web development, Open Source, business opportunities, etc. We look forward to your participation, and let's make this community to be one of the most popular GDG in the world!
Disclaimer: GDG Shanghai is an independent group; our activities and the opinions expressed on this +GDG Shanghai should in no way be linked to Google, the corporation.

赞助方

GDG DevFests are large scale community-run events that can offer speaker sessions across Google product areas, all-day hack-a-thons or both. Each GDG DevFest will be inspired by and uniquely tailored to the needs of the developer community that hosts it. But while no two events will be exactly alike, each GDG DevFest will, at its core, be powered by a shared belief that when developers come together to exchange ideas, amazing things can happen.
